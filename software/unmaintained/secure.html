The project description.

Privacy is an important thing. You should protect two things to keep the privacy: the internet connection and the computer. You can protect the internet connection using services like Tor or VPN. You can protect the computer in different ways: wiping free space, destroying your private data, or encrypting it. I made a few tools to help you protect the computer.
