Project description

Privacy is an important thing. You should protect two things to keep the privacy: the internet connection and the computer. You can protect the internet connection using services like Tor or VPN. You can protect the computer in different ways: wiping free space, destroying or encrypting the data. I made a few tools to help you protect the computer.
